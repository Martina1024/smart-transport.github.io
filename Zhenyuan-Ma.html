Zhenyuan Ma

Website

Zhenyuan Ma joined the smart transport group as a master student in January 2019 and she is going to graduate in January 2021. She got her Bachelor degree in computer science at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. She is interested in big data analysis, machine learning and algorithms, agent-based simulation and travel demand forecasting, and spatiotemporal analysis. Her master's thesis topic is multi-level population synthesis and missing value imputation on survey and census data.

Research Interests
