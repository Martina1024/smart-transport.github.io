Fuqiang Liu

Ph.D. candidate



Fuqiang received his bachelor’s and master’s degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China, in 2014 and 2016 respectively. Then he worked as a research assistant in deep learning acceleration at Clarkson University. From Sep 2019, he works as a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Lijun Sun and Prof. Luis Miranda-Moreno.

Research Interests

  • Robust intelligent transportation systems (ITS)
  • Internet-of-thing (IoT) systems
  • Spatiotemporal graph neural networks
  • Adversarial study
  • Reinforcement learning
