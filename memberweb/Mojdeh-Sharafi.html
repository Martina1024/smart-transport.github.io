Mojdeh Sharafi

M.Eng.

Website

Mojdeh is a M.Eng (thesis) student in Civil Engineering and Applied Mechanics atMcGill University. Prof. Lijun Sun is her supervisor, with Prof. Laxmi Sushama as her co-supervisor. Her research includes bike sharing and micro-mobility. She is currently investigating how location of bike sharing stations and non-spatial variables, like weather, can affect the ridership of bike sharing in Montreal, and how to model these type of parameters.
