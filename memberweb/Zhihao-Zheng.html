Zhihao (Vincent) Zheng

Ph.D. Student

Google Scholar Website

Vincent received his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Transportation Engineering from Central South University in 2017 and 2020. He joined the Smart Transport Group at McGill University in winter, 2021. He is currently working Prof. Lijun Sun on the subject of spatiotemporal modeling for urban mobility.

Research Interests

  • Spatiotemporal Data Modeling
  • Physcis-informed Deep Learning
  • Non-recurring Pattern Recognition and Prediction

Selected Publications

  • Zheng Z, et al. Hybrid model for predicting anomalous large passenger flow in urban metros. IET Intelligent Transport Systems, 2020, 14(14), 1987-1996.
  • Zheng, Z., et al. Understanding coupling dynamics of public transportation networks. EPJ Data Science, 2018, 7, 1-16.
