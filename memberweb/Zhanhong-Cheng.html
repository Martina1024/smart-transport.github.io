Zhanhong Cheng

Ph.D. candidate

Google Scholar

Zhanhong Cheng is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Civil Engineering at McGill University. He received his master’s and bachelor’s degree from Harbin Institute of Technology. His current research focuses on utilizing passengers’ behavior patterns for better transit operation, planning, and forecasting. His master’s thesis is about the effect of the route choice model on traffic paradox.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning
  • Data-driven transportation modeling
  • Sustainable transportation
