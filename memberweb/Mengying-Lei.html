Mengying Lei is a second year PhD student working on traffic data analysis at McGill University, supervised by Prof. Lijun Sun and Prof. Aurelie Labbe. She received her M.S. degree in pattern recognition from Beihang University, focusing on dynamic Granger causality detection and EEG decoding methods during there.

Research Interests

  • Gaussian process
  • Spatial-temporal data imputation and prediction
